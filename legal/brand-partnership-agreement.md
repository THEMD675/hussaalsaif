### 10.1 GAMR Compliance

- All content will comply with the regulations of the General Authority for Media Regulation (GAMR, formerly GCAM)
- Advertising content will be clearly disclosed per GAMR guidelines
- Content must respect Saudi cultural values and public morality standards

### 10.2 ZATCA Compliance

- All invoices will comply with ZATCA e-invoicing requirements (Fatoorah)
- VAT will be applied at the prevailing rate (currently 15%)

### 10.3 Content Standards

Content will not:

- Include unauthorized filming of individuals, children, or domestic workers

Both parties will retain copies of all published content for a minimum of 90 days as required by Saudi media regulations.
