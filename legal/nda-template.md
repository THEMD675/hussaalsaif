## 3. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 3.1 Confidential Information Includes

"Confidential Information" means any and all non-public information disclosed by either party to the other, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to:

- **Product Information:** Unreleased products, prototypes, formulations, designs, features, specifications, pricing, and launch dates
- **Campaign Information:** Marketing strategies, campaign concepts, creative briefs, release schedules, launch dates, and promotional plans
- **Business Information:** Financial data, customer lists, business plans, sales figures, partnership details, and strategic plans
- **Technical Information:** Proprietary technology, trade secrets, algorithms, and software
- **Creative Materials:** Unpublished content, drafts, designs, photography, and video materials
- **Personal Information:** Contact details, addresses, and personal data of individuals involved
- **Terms of Engagement:** Financial terms, compensation structures, and contractual arrangements between the parties

### 3.2 Confidential Information Does NOT Include

Information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party
- Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure (with documented evidence)
- Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
- Is disclosed to the Receiving Party by a third party who has no obligation of confidentiality
- Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order (with prior written notice to the Disclosing Party where legally permitted)
