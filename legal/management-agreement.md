## 2. APPOINTMENT AND SCOPE

### 2.1 Appointment

Talent hereby appoints Manager as her **exclusive** personal manager and representative for all matters related to her career as a content creator and public figure, as further described in this Agreement.

### 2.2 Scope of Management Services

Manager shall provide the following services:

**Brand Partnerships and Deals:**
- Source, negotiate, and secure brand partnership opportunities
- Review and negotiate all partnership contracts and terms
- Manage ongoing brand relationships and communications
- Ensure timely payments from brand partners
- Develop and maintain a rate card and pricing strategy

**Business Strategy:**
- Develop and execute Talent's overall business and monetization strategy
- Identify new revenue opportunities and business ventures
- Plan and manage content strategy across platforms
- Advise on platform growth and audience development
- Research market trends and competitive landscape

**Operations and Administration:**
- Handle all business communications and inquiries on behalf of Talent
- Manage scheduling, calendars, and deadlines for business commitments
- Coordinate with third-party service providers (photographers, videographers, stylists, etc.)
- Manage invoicing, payment tracking, and basic financial administration
- Maintain organized records of all agreements, deliverables, and payments

**Legal and Compliance:**
- Coordinate legal review of contracts with qualified attorneys
- Ensure compliance with Saudi GAMR regulations and licensing requirements
- Monitor advertising disclosure requirements
- Protect Talent's intellectual property and image rights

**Public Relations and Image:**
- Manage Talent's public image and brand positioning
- Handle media inquiries and press opportunities
- Manage crisis communications if needed
- Advise on public appearances and speaking engagements

### 2.3 Exclusions

This Agreement does NOT cover:
- Management of Talent's personal financial investments or personal assets
- Legal representation (Manager is not an attorney and will engage legal counsel when needed)
- Personal matters unrelated to Talent's professional career
- Creative direction of content (final creative decisions remain with Talent per Section 6)

## 3. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

### 3.1 Management Commission

Manager shall receive a commission on Talent's gross revenue as follows:

| Revenue Source | Commission Rate |
|---------------|----------------|
| Brand partnerships and sponsorships | [15-20]% |
| Content licensing deals | [15-20]% |
| Event appearances and speaking engagements | [15-20]% |
| Affiliate and commission-based income | [15-20]% |
| Product collaborations and co-branded products | [15-20]% |
| Digital products and courses (if applicable) | [10-15]% |
| Consulting and advisory work | [15-20]% |

### 3.2 Commission Calculation

- Commission is calculated on **gross revenue** received by Talent before taxes but after any platform fees or payment processing fees
- Commission is calculated on revenue from deals secured, negotiated, or facilitated by Manager
- Commission is NOT charged on:
  - Revenue from deals entered into before this Agreement (listed in Appendix A)
  - Personal gifts or non-commercial income
  - Income from Talent's personal investments unrelated to her content creator career
  - AdSense or platform monetization revenue (e.g., YouTube AdSense, TikTok Creator Fund) unless specifically negotiated through Manager's efforts

### 3.3 Payment of Commission

- Commission payments are due within [7/14] days of Talent receiving payment from the brand/client
- Manager will issue an invoice for each commission payment
- All commissions are subject to applicable VAT per Saudi ZATCA regulations

### 3.4 Commission on Deals in Progress

If this Agreement terminates, Manager is entitled to commission on:
- All deals fully executed before termination
- Deals in active negotiation at the time of termination that close within [90] days after termination (the "Tail Period")
- Ongoing deals with payment installments that were negotiated during the term

### 3.5 Expense Reimbursement

Manager shall be reimbursed for reasonable, pre-approved business expenses incurred on behalf of Talent, including but not limited to:
- Travel expenses for meetings with brands (if not covered by the brand)
- Legal fees for contract review
- Software and tools used for managing Talent's business
- Any other expenses approved in writing by Talent before they are incurred

Expenses exceeding SAR [500] require prior written approval from Talent.

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term

This Agreement is effective from [START DATE] for an initial period of [12/24] months (the "Initial Term").

### 4.2 Renewal

After the Initial Term, this Agreement automatically renews for successive [6/12]-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least [60] days before the end of the current term.

### 4.3 Termination by Either Party

Either party may terminate this Agreement:
- **Without cause:** By providing [60] days written notice to the other party
- **For cause:** Immediately upon written notice if the other party:
  - Materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure within [14] days of written notice
  - Engages in fraud, dishonesty, or gross misconduct
  - Becomes unable to perform their obligations due to incapacity

### 4.4 Termination by Talent

Talent may terminate immediately if:
- Manager fails to secure any brand deals within a [6]-month period (unless Talent has rejected reasonable opportunities)
- Manager discloses Talent's confidential information without authorization
- Manager acts in a way that materially damages Talent's reputation or career
- Manager has a conflict of interest that cannot be resolved (see Section 7)

### 4.5 Termination by Manager

Manager may terminate immediately if:
- Talent consistently refuses to fulfill commitments to brands secured by Manager, causing reputational harm to Manager
- Talent engages in illegal activity that puts Manager at legal risk
- Talent fails to pay commissions owed for more than [30] days after they are due

### 4.6 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Manager will provide an orderly transition of all active deals, contacts, and ongoing business matters
- Manager will deliver all documents, files, passwords, and materials related to Talent's business within [14] days
- Talent will pay all outstanding commissions owed, including the Tail Period commissions per Section 3.4
- Both parties will continue to honor confidentiality obligations per Section 8
- Manager will cease representing themselves as Talent's manager immediately

## 5. MANAGER'S OBLIGATIONS

### 5.1 Standard of Care

Manager shall perform services with the skill, care, and diligence expected of a professional talent manager, and shall at all times act in Talent's best interest.

### 5.2 Communication and Reporting

Manager shall:
- Provide Talent with a monthly summary of all business activities, inquiries, and revenue
- Communicate all partnership opportunities to Talent in a timely manner
- Be reasonably available for communication during Saudi business hours
- Respond to Talent's communications within [24] hours on business days

### 5.3 Financial Transparency

Manager shall:
- Maintain accurate records of all revenue, expenses, and commissions
- Provide quarterly financial reports detailing all income and commission payments
- Make records available for review by Talent or Talent's accountant upon reasonable request
- Never commingle Talent's funds with Manager's personal or business funds

### 5.4 No Unauthorized Commitments

Manager shall not:
- Sign contracts on Talent's behalf without prior approval (unless authority is granted per Section 6)
- Commit Talent to any obligation without her knowledge and consent
- Accept payment on Talent's behalf without prior arrangement
- Make public statements on Talent's behalf without prior approval

## 6. DECISION-MAKING AUTHORITY

### 6.1 Talent Retains Final Authority Over

- All creative decisions regarding content (concept, style, execution, posting)
- Personal brand identity and image direction
- Which partnerships and deals to accept or reject
- Public statements and personal opinions expressed
- Social media account access and passwords
- Whether to appear at events or accept speaking engagements

### 6.2 Manager Has Authority To

- Negotiate deal terms within parameters set by Talent
- Communicate with brands and agencies on Talent's behalf
- Decline unsolicited inquiries that clearly fall below Talent's minimum rates
- Schedule meetings and manage Talent's business calendar
- Engage third-party service providers within pre-approved budgets
- Handle day-to-day operational decisions that do not create obligations for Talent

### 6.3 Shared Decision-Making

The following require discussion and mutual agreement:
- Setting and adjusting rate cards and pricing strategy
- Entering new business verticals or revenue streams
- Selecting long-term brand ambassador partnerships (3+ months)
- Any commitment involving exclusivity
- Public relations strategy and crisis response
- Significant changes to content strategy or platform focus

### 6.4 Signing Authority

- [ ] Manager may sign contracts on Talent's behalf for deals under SAR [AMOUNT] that have been verbally approved by Talent
- [ ] Manager may NOT sign any contracts on Talent's behalf; all contracts require Talent's direct signature
- [ ] Manager may sign using power of attorney (a separate POA document must be executed and notarized)

## 7. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

### 7.1 Disclosure Obligation

Manager shall promptly disclose to Talent any situation that creates or could create a conflict of interest, including but not limited to:
- Managing other talent who may compete with Talent for the same brand deals
- Having a financial interest in any brand or company that approaches Talent for a partnership
- Receiving any compensation, gifts, or benefits from brands seeking to work with Talent (beyond standard industry courtesies)

### 7.2 Managing Other Talent

- [ ] Manager is permitted to manage other talent, provided there are no direct conflicts
- [ ] Manager is the exclusive manager for Talent only and will not manage other content creators in the same niche
- [ ] Manager may manage up to [X] other talent, with disclosure to Talent

### 7.3 Resolution

If a conflict of interest arises:
1. Manager must disclose immediately in writing
2. Parties will discuss and attempt to resolve within [7] days
3. If unresolvable, Talent may terminate per Section 4.4

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 8.1 Manager's Obligations

Manager agrees to keep confidential:
- Talent's personal information, addresses, and private contacts
- Financial details including earnings, rates, and payment information
- Business strategy and plans not yet public
- Communications between Talent and Manager
- Information received from brands under NDA
- Talent's account credentials and passwords

### 8.2 Talent's Obligations

Talent agrees to keep confidential:
- Manager's commission rates and business terms
- Manager's proprietary business methods and industry contacts
- Information about Manager's other clients (if applicable)

### 8.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for [24] months, except for trade secrets which remain confidential indefinitely.
