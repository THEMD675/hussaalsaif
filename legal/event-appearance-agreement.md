## 8. CANCELLATION AND RESCHEDULING

### 8.1 Cancellation by Organizer

| Notice Period | Financial Consequence |
|--------------|----------------------|
| More than 30 days before event | Full refund minus booking deposit (50%) |
| 15-30 days before event | 75% of total fee retained by Talent |
| Less than 15 days before event | 100% of total fee retained by Talent |

### 8.2 Cancellation by Talent

| Notice Period | Financial Consequence |
|--------------|----------------------|
| More than 14 days before event | Full refund of all payments |
| 7-14 days before event | Full refund minus 25% cancellation fee |
| Less than 7 days before event | Full refund minus 50% cancellation fee |

### 8.3 Rescheduling

- Organizer may reschedule once, subject to Talent's availability, with at least 14 days notice
- If the rescheduled date does not work for Talent, cancellation terms in Section 8.1 apply
- Additional rescheduling requests are treated as cancellations and re-bookings

### 8.4 Force Majeure

Neither party is liable for cancellation due to force majeure events including but not limited to: natural disasters, government orders, public health emergencies, or security threats. In such cases:
- All fees paid will be applied to a rescheduled date within 90 days
- If rescheduling is not possible, payments will be refunded minus reasonable expenses already incurred

### 8.5 No-Show by Talent

If Talent fails to appear without valid cause or prior notice, Organizer is entitled to a full refund of all fees paid.
