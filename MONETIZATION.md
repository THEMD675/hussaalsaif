## 7. PRICING METHODOLOGY

### How These Rates Were Calculated

**Base Rate Formula:**
1. Start with global benchmark for 100K-300K follower beauty creators (USD 2,000-8,000 per Instagram post)
2. Apply GCC/KSA market multiplier: +15% (KSA rates run 100-120% of US rates)
3. Apply engagement rate premium: +30% (8.2% vs 1.5-3.5% industry average)
4. Apply beauty niche premium: +20% (beauty creators earn 30-40% more than general)
5. Apply premium brand portfolio factor: +10% (proven luxury brand partnerships signal quality)

**Rate Justification:**
- An 8.2% engagement rate is 2-3x the industry average. Brands pay for eyeballs that actually engage.
- Previous work with Sephora, Fendi, and Estee Lauder establishes market positioning alongside luxury players.
- Saudi national in KSA = premium value for any brand targeting the Kingdom under Vision 2030.
- Dual-city presence (Khobar + Riyadh) doubles geographic reach for event-based campaigns.

**When to Negotiate Down:**
- Long-term retainer (already discounted 25-60% in ambassador packages)
- Brand gifting of high-value products (> SAR 2,000) can offset 10-15%
- Affiliate/commission hybrid (reduce base by 15-20% and add 8-12% revenue share)
- Emerging brand with growth potential and guaranteed exclusivity

**When to Negotiate Up:**
- Category exclusivity requested: +SAR 7,500/month
- Rush turnaround (< 48 hours): +30%
- Usage rights for paid advertising: +50-100%
- Whitelisting through creator account: +50-75%
- International travel required: minimum SAR 56,250 + all expenses
- Perpetual content rights: +100% of base

### Regulatory Note

Influencers advertising goods or services on social media in Saudi Arabia must register with the General Authority for Audiovisual Media Regulation (GAMR) and obtain an Influencer Licence (~SAR 15,000 / USD 4,000 for a three-year licence). Factor this into annual operating costs.
