### What Agencies Charge

**Agency fees (campaign-based):**
- Campaign management fees: typically 15-30% on top of influencer costs
- Minimum campaign budgets: SAR 20,000-100,000+ depending on agency
- Retainer models: SAR 10,000-50,000/month for ongoing management

**Talent management (if Hussa signs with one):**
- Standard commission: 15-25% of all brand deal revenue
- Some agencies take up to 30-40% for smaller creators
- Contract lock-in: typically 1-3 years

### Should Hussa Sign With an Agency or Stay Independent?

**RECOMMENDATION: STAY INDEPENDENT -- for now.**

**Why independent is better at this stage:**

1. **Hussa already has a professional website** -- most creators at her tier don't. She doesn't need an agency to look legitimate.
2. **Higher margins** -- keeping 100% of brand deal revenue vs. giving away 15-25%
3. **Full creative control** -- agencies push creators toward whatever brands pay the most, not what fits their brand
4. **The website IS the management layer** -- hussaalsaif.com with a proper media kit, rate card, and brand inquiry form replaces what an agency provides
5. **Saudi market favors direct relationships** -- brands in KSA often prefer working directly with creators

**When to consider an agency (later):**
- When she's consistently getting 5+ brand inquiries per month and can't handle negotiations
- When she wants to break into international markets (UAE, Kuwait, global brands)
- When she's ready to scale beyond content into events, TV, or large campaigns
- At 200K+ followers, OMNES Influencers or a similar talent management firm could add value

**Hybrid approach (best of both worlds):**
- Stay independent for day-to-day management
- Register on platforms like Collabstr, Modash, or HypeAuditor to be discoverable by brands
- Use the hussaalsaif.com website as the primary business hub
- Consider a freelance manager/assistant before a full agency

## 4. REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

### Mawthooq License (Critical for Hussa)

- **What:** GCAM (General Commission for Audiovisual Media) requires all influencers earning income from advertising to hold a "Mawthooq" (verified) license
- **Cost:** SAR 15,000 (~$4,000)
- **Duration:** Valid for 3 years
- **Penalty for non-compliance:** Fines up to SAR 5 million
- **Requirement:** Must flag/disclose all advertising content
- **Status:** Hussa needs this if she's doing any paid promotions in KSA
