## 4. CONTENT FORMAT RECOMMENDATIONS

### Highest Performing Formats in Beauty/Lifestyle (2026)

**1. GRWM (Get Ready With Me) - THE dominant format**
- Combine storytelling with product application
- Works across ALL platforms
- Eid GRWM, wedding GRWM, daily GRWM
- Average 3-5x higher engagement than standard tutorials

**2. "Worth the Hype?" Product Reviews**
- Fast-paced, opinion-led reviews
- 15-30 seconds on TikTok, 30-60 on Reels
- Use on-screen text ratings and verdict overlays
- Drives saves (algorithm gold) and shares (DM distribution)

**3. Carousel Edu-tainment (Instagram)**
- 8-10 slide educational carousels
- "5 ingredients to avoid," "SPF myths debunked," "Books that changed me"
- Highest save rate of any Instagram format
- Positions Hussa as an authority, not just a face

**4. Photo Dump / Lifestyle Carousels**
- Casual, curated photo collections
- Eid moments, travel days, aesthetic daily life
- Feels authentic, drives comments ("where is this?", "outfit details?")
- Low production cost, high engagement

**5. Duet/Stitch Reactions (TikTok)**
- React to viral beauty trends, product claims, or BookTok content
- Adds Hussa's personality and expertise to trending conversations
- Rides existing algorithmic momentum

**6. Long-Form YouTube (Reading Room + Deep Dives)**
- 10-20 minute videos for YouTube
- Monthly book reviews, seasonal beauty routines, brand trip vlogs
- Builds deep audience loyalty and YouTube ad revenue
- Repurpose clips into 8-10 Shorts

**7. Interactive Stories (Instagram/Snapchat)**
- Polls, quizzes, "this or that," Q&As
- Algorithm loves story engagement
- Use for audience research and content planning

### Production Notes
- Always add on-screen text/subtitles (silent viewers = majority of audience)
- Original audio only for Instagram Reels (algorithm penalizes TikTok watermarks)
- Re-edit TikTok content natively for Reels - never cross-post with watermark
- Hook in first 1.5 seconds or lose them
