## TIMING RULES

| Action | Timing |
|--------|--------|
| Initial outreach | Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient's local time |
| Follow-Up 1 | 5-7 business days after initial email |
| Follow-Up 2 | 7-10 business days after Follow-Up 1 |
| Follow-Up 3 | 14-21 business days after Follow-Up 2 |
| After Follow-Up 3 (no response) | STOP. Mark as "revisit in 3-6 months" |
| Re-engage after 3-6 months | Fresh email (new thread), reference something new and timely |

**Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**Best time:** 9-11 AM recipient's local time (KSA: GST+3, UAE: GST+4, Europe: CET/GMT)
**Worst days:** Sunday (start of work week in GCC -- inbox overload), Friday (weekend in GCC)
**Never send:** After 3 PM local time, on weekends, or on Saudi/GCC public holidays

## WHAT TO DO WHEN THEY REPLY

**If they reply positively:**
- Respond within 4 hours (same business day)
- Share media kit immediately
- Propose 2-3 specific call times within the next 48 hours
- Switch to Template from inbound-response.md (Template 2 -- clear brief) once you have details

**If they reply with "not right now":**
- Thank them genuinely
- Ask: "Would it be helpful if I reached out again in Q[X]? I'd love to stay on your radar for future campaigns."
- Set a calendar reminder for the date they suggest (or 3 months if they don't specify)
- Do NOT follow up again until that date

**If they reply asking for rates only:**
- Use inbound-response.md Template 3
- Don't just send a rate card without context -- always include the media kit and a brief note on why the partnership makes sense

**If they reply forwarding you to someone else:**
- Thank the original contact
- Email the new contact within 24 hours
- Reference who forwarded you: "Hi [New Contact], [Original Contact] suggested I reach out to you regarding..."
- Start fresh with the initial outreach template (adjusted for the new context)
