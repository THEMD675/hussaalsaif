## RESPONSE RULES

1. **Always respond within 24 hours.** Even if it's just "Thanks for reaching out, I'll send a full response by [date]."
2. **Always ask for the brief.** Don't agree to anything without understanding the full scope.
3. **Always attach the media kit.** Every response should include it.
4. **Never accept gifting-only from brands that can afford to pay.** Sephora, Fendi, L'Oreal brands, LVMH brands -- they have budgets. Gifting-only is acceptable from small startups or local businesses only.
5. **Move DM conversations to email immediately.** DMs are informal and untrackable. Email creates a paper trail.
6. **Never commit to deliverables or timelines in the first response.** Get the full picture first.
7. **Always suggest a call.** Email negotiations take forever. A 15-minute call closes deals faster.
8. **Reference specific brand/product knowledge.** Even in a response, show you know the brand. It reinforces credibility.
