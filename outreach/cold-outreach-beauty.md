## CUSTOMIZATION NOTES

**Before sending ANY email:**

1. **Research the contact.** Find their name, role, and LinkedIn. Never send to "Dear Team" or "To Whom It May Concern."
2. **Reference something specific.** A recent Instagram post, a product launch, an event. Prove you actually pay attention to the brand.
3. **Match the brand's tone.** Charlotte Tilbury is glamorous and bold. Rare Beauty is soft and authentic. NARS is edgy and confident. Adjust your language accordingly.
4. **Attach nothing in the first email.** Mention the media kit but don't attach it -- attachments trigger spam filters on new domains. Send it in the reply thread.
5. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM KSA time** (which is 9-11 AM GST for UAE contacts, 8-10 AM for Europe).

**Contact research approach for beauty brands:**
- Check the brand's Instagram bio for PR/collab email
- Search LinkedIn for "[Brand Name] Middle East PR" or "[Brand Name] GCC Influencer Marketing"
- Check the brand's regional website for a press or partnerships page
- For L'Oreal brands (MAC, Urban Decay, Kerastase, Redken, YSL Beauty): contact L'Oreal Middle East PR team
- For Estee Lauder brands (MAC, Too Faced, Clinique, La Mer): contact Estee Lauder Companies ME
- For LVMH brands (Benefit, Givenchy, Fenty): contact LVMH ME PR
