## CONTACT RESEARCH APPROACH

**Step 1: Find the right person**
- Search LinkedIn: "[Brand Name] Middle East" + "PR" or "Influencer" or "Marketing"
- Check brand's Instagram bio for PR/collaboration email
- Check brand's regional website for press/partnerships page
- For parent company brands, find the ME regional PR team (they cover all sub-brands)

**Step 2: Verify the email**
- Use Hunter.io (free tier: 25 searches/month) to find email patterns
- Use Apollo.io (free tier) for contact enrichment
- Use Snov.io (free tier: 50 credits/month) for email verification
- Pattern testing: try firstname@brand.com, firstname.lastname@brand.com, first.last@brand-me.com

**Step 3: Before sending**
- Verify the email is valid (use Snov.io or NeverBounce to check)
- Confirm the person is still at the company (check LinkedIn activity)
- Check if the brand is currently running influencer campaigns (check their tagged posts on Instagram)
